About Jay Argall

I'm a 23 year old mechanical engineering grad (2016) who likes writing science fiction and fantasy. I also like drawing comics, creating digital art, and conlanging.

I'm interested in lots of things, the biggest being space - most of the science fiction I write is set in space, or at the very least, takes place on several different planets.

I've also got a heavy interest in linguistics, which is related to my conlanging. Conlangs often show up in my work, as does multilingualism, even though I'm not multilingual myself. Men ska jag vara någon dag! Jag försöker att lära mig svenska.

You can typically find me on Twitter, Tumblr, and Wordpress. Twitter is my main social media site. I post my artwork and conlang information on Tumblr. And Wordpress is my blog.
