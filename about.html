About Jay Argall

I'm a 23 year old CAD drafter/detailer who likes writing science fiction and fantasy. I also like drawing comics, creating digital art, and conlanging.

About the Website

As of 11 March 2018, this is the third iteration of this GitHub website. It was first created in October 2016 to act as a repository of all my creative works - writing, art, and conlangs.

The Contact page lists the websites I use semi-regularly and my username on each. The link called Blog goes to my Wordpress blog, which is where my general writing & other updates go. The Works page gives brief summaries and links to the creative work that I've posted online. The Twitter link goes to my Twitter. Twitter is my main social media site, though I only check it once every few days.

I plan on putting up a page on my conlangs sometime in the near future.
